## Summary
This review synthesizes recent advancements in VLA models, which unify perception, language understanding, and embodied action within a single framework. The work outlines five thematic pillars covering architectural innovations, training strategies, and real-time inference. It explores diverse applications including autonomous vehicles, medical robotics, and humanoid robots while addressing critical challenges such as multimodal action representation and system scalability. Key solutions proposed include agentic AI adaptation and unified neuro-symbolic planning to enhance socially aligned, adaptive agents.

## Key Points
