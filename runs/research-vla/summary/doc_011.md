## Summary
XL-VLA is a framework that learns a shared latent action space across dexterous hands, enabling single policy training on pooled multi-hand demonstrations and transfer to new hand-task configurations. It utilizes unsupervised latent autoencoders to decode unified actions into joint trajectories, achieving strong zero-shot generalization and cross-embodiment scaling similar to existing VLA baselines.

## Key Points
- Learns a shared latent action space across diverse dexterous hands.
- Enables single policy training on pooled multi-hand demonstrations.
- Achieves strong zero-shot generalization to unseen hand-task combinations.
- Demonstrates cross-embodiment scaling with latent actions.
