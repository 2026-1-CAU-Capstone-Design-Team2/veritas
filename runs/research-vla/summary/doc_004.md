## Summary
This paper revisits the integration of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models into existing Vision-Language-Models (VLMs), addressing limitations in action planning and reasoning capabilities. The authors propose a novel framework that enhances VLMs with a unified architecture for visual understanding, language comprehension, and multi-modal reasoning.

## Key Points
- The paper introduces a unified architecture to integrate vision-language-action capabilities into existing VLMs.
